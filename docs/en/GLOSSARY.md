# Institutional Glossary

Translation status: synced

## Supporter

A person who contributes financially, promotes or supports the initiative without automatically becoming a contributor, maintainer or cooperative member.

## Community member

A person who participates in discussions, prioritization and community activities.

## Contributor

A person who contributes with code, documentation, translation, research, testing, design, education or community organization.

## Maintainer

A person responsible for reviewing contributions, organizing repositories and protecting project integrity.

## Future cooperative member

A person who may join a future formal cooperative structure, subject to legal and statutory validation.

## Common good

A shared resource collectively cared for and oriented toward community benefit.

## Incubation

A stage in which a project is matured before becoming an official SaaSCoop project.

## Official project

A project accepted as a permanent part of the SaaSCoop ecosystem.
